Many manufacturers offer free or paid options for extended warranties, but they know that after 2 or 3 years very few people claim on these offers.
Use Life tracker to keep a record of purchase date, purchase place, warranty included and warranty expiration date.
Get reminders when the warranty offered will expire, is your device still working as expected? If not get value for money by contacting the manufacturer before the expiration date.
